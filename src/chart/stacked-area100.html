This sample demonstrates 100 percent stacked area series type in the chart.

Source: www.gov.uk

In this example, you can see how to render and configure the 100% stacked area type charts. Stacks the series on top of another series to avoid the overlapping of series with one another, when rendering more than one area series in same chart. You can use fill properties to customize the 100% stacked area.


Injecting Module

Chart component features are segregated into individual feature-wise modules. To use 100% stacked area series, we need to inject StackingAreaService into the @NgModule.providers section.

More information on the 100% stacked area series can be found in this documentation section.
